# 📊 Customer Behavior & Sales Insights Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to uncover customer purchasing patterns, sales trends, payment preferences, and regional performance.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that can support strategic decision-making, customer retention, and revenue growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify sales and revenue trends
- Understand payment preferences
- Discover top-performing regions
- Segment customers based on spending patterns
- Generate actionable business recommendations

## 🔍 Key Findings

### Revenue Performance

- Total Revenue Generated: **16M+**
- Strong revenue growth observed during peak shopping periods.

### Payment Preferences

- Credit Card was the most preferred payment method.
- Majority of transactions were completed through card payments.

### Geographic Insights

Top Revenue Generating States:

1. SP (São Paulo)
2. RJ (Rio de Janeiro)
3. MG (Minas Gerais)

### Customer Insights

- High-value customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue.
- Revenue distribution follows a customer concentration pattern where a small percentage of customers generate a large share of sales.
